## [0.0.1] - 11-03-2026

### Added
- **Initial Release**: Introducing `mpv_audio_kit`, a cross-platform audio player for Flutter powered by the robust `libmpv` engine built upon the latest `mpv` v0.41.0.
- **Cross-Platform Support**: Seamless audio playback out-of-the-box on `iOS`, `Android`, `macOS`, `Windows`, and `Linux`.
- **Advanced DSP & Pitch Control**: Live and programmatic access to professional audio effects, including real-time pitch shifting (without affecting tempo) and scalable multi-band Parametric EQ.
- **Hardware Output Selection**: Dynamically detect and switch between physical Audio Devices (e.g., Bluetooth headphones, external DACs) without interrupting playback.
- **Uninterrupted Gapless Playback**: Professional music player streaming experience with true native Gapless queues.
- **Comprehensive Format & Network Support**: Native support for virtually all audio codecs and streaming formats including HLS, DASH, and custom HTTP Headers overriding.
- **Complete Test Application**: A fully fledged `example` music app demonstrating all features, including hardware info routing and playback queues.
